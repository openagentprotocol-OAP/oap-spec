# Code of Conduct

The Open Agent Protocol community is committed to providing a harassment free, inclusive, and respectful environment for every participant, regardless of background, identity, or affiliation.

## Expected behavior

1. Be respectful, constructive, and patient.
2. Assume good faith and seek to understand before disagreeing.
3. Focus on technical merit, evidence, and the public interest.
4. Welcome new contributors and help them succeed.

## Unacceptable behavior

1. Personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination on any basis.
2. Sustained disruption of discussion or governance processes.
3. Publication of private information without consent.
4. Deceptive practices, including astroturfing or manipulation of governance signals.

## Reporting

Concerns may be reported to the Foundation Conduct Committee at `conduct@openagentprotocol.org`. Reports are handled confidentially. Reporters are protected under the Foundation Whistleblower Program from retaliation.

## Enforcement

The Conduct Committee may, after due process, issue warnings, suspend participation, or revoke contributor privileges. Decisions may be appealed to the Foundation Board.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is informed by established norms in standards organizations and open source projects, adapted to the OAP governance structure.
